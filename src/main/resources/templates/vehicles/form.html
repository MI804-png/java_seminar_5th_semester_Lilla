Field Information

Registration Number

  • Exactly 6 alphanumeric characters (A-Z, 0-9)
  • This is the unique identifier for the vehicle
  • Cannot be changed after vehicle creation
  • Must match a person's registration number to link them

Vehicle Details

  • Brand: Vehicle manufacturer (max 20 characters)
  • Color: Vehicle color (max 20 characters)
  • All fields are required
  • Select from predefined colors or choose "Other"
Linking Vehicles to Persons

To link a vehicle to a person (owner), both must have the same registration number. You can create the person first, then create the vehicle with matching registration number, or vice versa.
