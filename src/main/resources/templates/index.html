About Our System

The Vehicle Registration Management System is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline vehicle registration processes. Built with modern technologies including Java Spring Boot, Spring Security, and MySQL, our system provides a secure and efficient way to manage vehicle records, owner information, and related data.

Whether you're an administrator managing the entire database or a registered user updating your information, our system offers an intuitive interface and powerful features to meet your needs.
